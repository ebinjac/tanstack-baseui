# Team Registration

Navigate to **`/teams/register`** to open the registration wizard. The form is divided into **4 steps**, each validated before allowing you to proceed.

---

## Step 1 — Team Details

| Field | Required | Notes |
|-------|----------|-------|
| **Team Name** | ✅ Yes | Must be unique. Availability is checked in real-time as you type. A green checkmark ✓ confirms the name is available. |
| **Description & Notes** | No | Provide context to help administrators approve your request quickly. |

> [!TIP]
> Team names must be at least 3 characters long before availability is checked. Wait for the spinner to disappear before clicking **Continue**.

---

## Step 2 — Access Control

| Field | Required | Notes |
|-------|----------|-------|
| **User Active Directory Group** | ✅ Yes | e.g., `ENSEMBLE-PE-USERS`. All members of this group will have standard access. |
| **Admin Active Directory Group** | ✅ Yes | e.g., `ENSEMBLE-PE-ADMINS`. Members of this group will have administrative privileges within the team. |

> [!CAUTION]
> Ensure both AD groups are **currently active** in your corporate directory before submitting. Inactive groups will cause access provisioning to fail.

---

## Step 3 — Contact Information

| Field | Required | Notes |
|-------|----------|-------|
| **Primary Point of Contact** | ✅ Yes | The full name of the person responsible for this team on the platform. |
| **Contact Email Address** | ✅ Yes | A valid corporate email. Confirmation and updates will be sent here. |

---

## Step 4 — Review & Submit

This step displays a summary of all information you entered. Review each field carefully before submitting.

A **"Final Validation"** notice reminds you that by submitting, you confirm all details are correct and authorized.

Click **"Submit Registration"** to send the request for admin review.

### After Submission

Once submitted, you will see a success screen confirming:
- Your request has been sent for review
- A confirmation email was sent to your contact address
- You will be notified once an administrator approves the request
